About

Michael Bublé, born September 9th 1975, is a Canadian-Italian singer, songwriter, actor, and record producer. The first album he released achieved top ten in Canada and the UK.

Over the course of his career, Michael Bublé has released ten studio albums, three live albums, nine EPs, seventeen singles, and fourteen music videos.

Find out more about Michael Bublé on his website.

Awards

Michael Bublé has won a number of awards, including but not limited to:

  • Album of the Year - 2006, 2010, 2012
  • Fan Choice Award - 2007, 2011, 2015
  • Pop Album of the Year - 2006, 2010
  • Single of the Year - 2006, 2009
  • Artist of the Year - 2006
